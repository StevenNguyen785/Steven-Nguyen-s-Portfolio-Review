Artist Statement

Welcome to My Official Artist Portfolio Website!

This website shows digital media arts from digital imagery to web development from my very own collection.The software applications that I use ranges from Adobe Photoshop to Audacity. With the use of these groundbreaking digital artworks from which I have created, I will show a digital online art exhibition that will give the audience or viewer a combined emotional feelings of color, humor, exhilaration, and most of all entertainment. You will explore a variety of mediums from different sounds to Virtual Reality. During the development stages of working on these projects, I have came to an epiphany of what my interest and future goals would be. My main interests are computer programming and video production. Besdes reaching my dream goal as becoming a professional digital media artist, I want to motivate and help out other artist and non-artist alike. Famous digital media artist that have< inspired me to pursue my dream are Takeshi Murata, Nam Jun Paik, and Mary Ellen Bute. If someone asks me what is my “ultimate” goal of becoming an artist would be which is becoming a movie director so therefore famous film directors that have inspired me to pursue in video editing and film production are James Wan, Justin Lin, and Christopher Nolan.

I hope you enjoy exploring my digital artworks and THANKS FOR VISITING MY PORTFOLIO WEBSITE!
